====
Team
====

Gradient-Free-Optimizers is created and maintained by Simon Blanke with contributions
from the open-source community.

----

Core Team
---------

**Simon Blanke** - Creator & Maintainer

- GitHub: `@SimonBlanke <https://github.com/SimonBlanke>`_
- Email: simon.blanke@yahoo.com
- LinkedIn: `Simon Blanke <https://www.linkedin.com/in/simon-blanke-b6228a1b3/>`_

Simon created GFO in 2019 as part of a larger effort to make gradient-free optimization
more accessible. He continues to maintain the project, review contributions, and guide
its development.
